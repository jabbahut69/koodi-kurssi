MAP OF CASTLE SAINSBURY

The Sainsburies have lived in the Sainsbury castle for generations. The castle was first built somewhere in the 16th century, but has been expanded and renovetad several times since.

The main entrance lies on the Northern side of tha castle. It leads to the Outer Court, which makes way to the more intimate spaces of the castle. You will find the old Sally Port to the West.
